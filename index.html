The Rewa White Tiger Reserve in Madhya Pradesh, India, holds a unique place in the conservation and historical legacy of the rare white tiger. This reserve, known as the birthplace of Mohan, the first white tiger spotted in the wild, was established to honor and protect these majestic animals.
